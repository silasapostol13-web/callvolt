Video Script

2-Minute Demo Video

Record this on your phone or with a screen recorder. Send to prospects who reply "yes" to your outreach. This is your highest-converting sales asset.

⏱ Total Length: 1:50 - 2:10
🎬 Format: Face + Screen Share
📱 Record With: Loom, Phone, or Zoom
0:00 The Hook ~15 sec
🎥
Camera — Your Face

Look into camera. Casual, confident. No fancy background needed — your truck, office, or just a clean wall works.

YOU (voiceover / on camera):
"Hey [First Name] — Silas here. I wanted to show you something quick. You know those calls that come in when you're on a job, or at 9 PM on a Saturday? Let me show you what happens when an AI agent picks up instead of voicemail."
💡 Personalize the first name. Even if you batch-record, re-record this 5-second intro per prospect. Personal = 3x higher watch rate.
0:15 The Live Demo ~50 sec
🖥️
Screen Share — Interactive Demo

Open the AI Demo HTML file in your browser. Walk through a call scenario while narrating. Pick the scenario that matches their industry.

YOU (voiceover while clicking through demo):
"So a customer calls about [emergency/service relevant to their business]. The AI picks up immediately — no hold music, no 'press 1 for this, press 2 for that.'

It greets them naturally: 'Thank you for calling [their business name], this is Alex, how can I help?'

Then it asks the right qualifying questions — [mention 1-2 specific questions for their industry]. It's not just taking a message. It's actually diagnosing the issue and booking the appointment.

[Click through to the result screen]

And look at that — call handled, job booked, customer confirmed. Total time: under 2 minutes. And this is happening 24/7, whether you're asleep, on a job, or on vacation."
💡 Use THEIR business name in the demo if possible. Hearing their own company name in the AI's greeting is an "oh wow" moment that sells harder than anything else you could say.
1:05 The Math ~25 sec
🖥️
Screen Share — ROI Calculator

Open the ROI calculator. Adjust sliders to roughly match their business size.

YOU:
"Now here's the part that matters — the math. If [their business] gets about [X] calls a month and you're missing even 20% of them... that's [X] missed calls. At your average ticket of [$X], you're leaving [$X,XXX] on the table every single month.

The AI captures about 85% of those. That's [$X,XXX] in recovered revenue — for a fraction of what you'd pay a part-time receptionist."
1:30 The Close ~20 sec
🎥
Camera — Back to Your Face

Look into camera. Warm, direct. No pressure.

YOU:
"Setup takes less than a week, no contract, and you don't have to touch any technology — I handle everything.

If you want to see it live with your actual business name and services, just reply to this and I'll set up a quick 10-minute call. No pitch — I'll literally just call your AI agent while you listen.

Talk soon, [First Name]."
💡 End with a SPECIFIC next step — "reply to this" or "text me at [number]." Generic CTAs like "reach out whenever" don't convert.
— END OF SCRIPT —

Recording Checklist

Quick sanity check before you hit record:

Good lighting (face a window if using phone)
Demo HTML files open and ready in browser tabs
ROI calculator pre-set with their approximate numbers
Their business name written down to say naturally
Record in landscape if screen-sharing
Keep energy up but conversational — not salesy
Total length: aim for under 2 minutes (shorter = better watch rate)
Upload to Loom for a clean link, or text/email the video directly
