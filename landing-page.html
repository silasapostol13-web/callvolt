Common Questions

Does it actually sound like a real person? ▾
Yes. Modern AI voice technology is remarkably natural-sounding. Most customers don't realize they're talking to an AI — and even if they do, they prefer it over voicemail. We train the agent on your business's specific tone and services.
Do I need to change my phone number? ▾
No. We set up call forwarding from your existing number. Your customers call the same number they always have. The AI handles what you can't — after hours, overflow, and busy times.
What if the AI can't handle a call? ▾
The AI is configured to recognize complex situations and escalate appropriately — either transferring to you live or sending you an immediate text alert. You stay in control of everything that matters.
How long does setup take? ▾
Less than a week from kickoff to live. The setup process requires about 30 minutes of your time — filling out a questionnaire about your business. We handle everything else.
Is there a long-term contract? ▾
No contracts. Month-to-month. We keep you because the results speak for themselves, not because of fine print. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
What results should I expect? ▾
Most clients see 10–20 additional booked jobs per month within the first 30 days — calls that were previously going to voicemail or competitors. At typical service ticket values, that's $2,000–$5,000+ in recovered monthly revenue.
